The Orchestration

Four simple steps from a snapshot of your room to an interactive knowledge portal.

01

Scan

The AI instantly recognizes everyday items in your photo and maps them to educational artifacts.

02

Analyze

Objects are transformed into knowledge anchors -- a mug becomes a medieval chalice, a bookshelf turns into an evidence board.

03

Generate

A full 360° learning environment is generated with interactive hotspots, quizzes, and a voice-guided narrative.

04

Explore

Enter your transformed space. Use voice to interact with a knowledge guide and solve educational challenges.
